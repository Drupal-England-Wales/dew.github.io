The specific mechanism for recording votes as not yet been decided upon, Rachel Lawson is currently investigating options. The intended election program, minuted here, is:

13th to 21st March - Call for Board candidates
21st to 27th March - Candidate campaigning to members
27th to 30th March - Online voting takes place
31st March - Chairperson and Board members announced

The method for voting and counting will be “instant run-off voting”. The outright winner will be appointed Chairperson and the next six successful candidates will be appointed Board members, role to be determined at the next meeting of the Board.
